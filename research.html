Research Interests

My current research is mainly focused on the applications of machine learning in healthcare. I am working on a few COVID-19 projects at the moment including using proteomics to predict adverse COVID-19 outcomes and using chest X-rays to predict COVID-19 patient deterioration.

My broad interests are in:

  • Applications of AI and Machine Learning to improve health care and clinical decision-making
  • Statistical Genetics
  • Causal Inference
